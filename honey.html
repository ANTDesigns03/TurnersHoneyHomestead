    Autumn

    In the autumn season the nector collected from bees is typically amber in color resulting in a darker honey color. The taste is also described as a earthier and heartier flavor. Below are some flowers that you can plant to get this sharper flavors.

    • Winter

    In the winter the honey is usually the darkest in color. Beekeepers typically harvest the honey but leave enough for the bees to survive the winter months. The flavors are based on late summer/fall notes and super hearty.
