    Fun Facts

    In a single hive, it is made up of Worker (female) bees, Drone (male) bees and the Queen.

    Honey bees fly at 25km per hour and beat their wings 200 times per second!

    If the Queen bee dies they feed royal honey to a few young larva to make more.

    The average lifespan for a typicall working honey bee is six weeks.

    One bee has 170 ordor receptors that help them communicate and look for food sources.
